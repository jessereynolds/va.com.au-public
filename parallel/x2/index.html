PARALLEL presents cross-disciplinary work from artists and writers. It contributes to the on-going research into web practices and theories.

PARALLEL covers topics including architecture, art, fiction, design, video, film, language, sexualities, death, writing...

Our enablers are Camtech (SA) and Apple Australia.
