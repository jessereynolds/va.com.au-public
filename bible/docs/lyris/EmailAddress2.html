Email Address

(required) Defines the email address that the Auto-Responder will respond to. If you enter "sales", any mail sent to "sales@…" on this Lyris Server will trigger the Auto-Responder. You can not have duplicate Auto-Responder email addresses on the same site. If you choose to have duplicate Auto-Responders for different sites, Lyris will use the Site hostname to determine which Auto-Responder is appropriate.

When Lyris replies to a auto-responder request, the From: and Reply-To: of the document sent back is the address of the auto-responder itself.
