Held Notify Interval

When a member bounces too much email, their status can be changed to "held" that means that their email address has been declared as "invalid" or "inoperational" and they no longer receive list postings.

Lyris has the capability to send a notification message to people on hold, to tell them about their status, and give them the option of un-holding themselves. A member can unhold themselves by sending email to "unhold@…" at your Lyris server.

This setting determines the frequency at which Lyris notifies held members. You can choose to never notify held members, notify them every night, every few days, or whatever interval you choose.
