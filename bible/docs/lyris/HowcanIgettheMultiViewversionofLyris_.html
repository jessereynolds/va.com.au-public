How can I get the MultiView version of Lyris?

    I'm interested in MultiView. When I went to the web download and the ftp site, I didn't see anything that distinguished them apart. Is there any difference (other than the license key)?

The Lyris MultiView features are included as part of the version of Lyris that you download from our web site. The various levels of Lyris, including the MultiView features, are activated by a serial number. A limited version of MultiView works with the free version of Lyris, without any serial number.

To download Lyris, go to http://www.lyris.com/down/
