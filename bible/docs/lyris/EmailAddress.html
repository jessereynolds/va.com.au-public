Email Address

If you have more members on your mailing list than can be displayed on the screen at once, an extra line will appear, with an "email address" field, "alphabetical" field, and "go to" button. This allows you to jump directly to a specific email address or pattern match. For example, if you type "patrick@somewhere.com", and click the "Go To" button, Lyris will jump to a member named "patrick@somewhere.com", or if that name does not appear, will jump to the alphabetically nearest name. If you are not certain how to spell an email address, you can enter a fragment, such as "pat", and Lyris will go to the alphabetically nearest email address to that fragment.
