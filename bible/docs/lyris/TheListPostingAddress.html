The List Posting Address

Email Address: Listname@

Purpose: the address for sending mail to all the members of a mailing list. For example, if your list is called "jazztalk" and your server is named "clio.lyris.net", then you can submit postings to your list by sending email to jazztalk@clio.lyris.net
