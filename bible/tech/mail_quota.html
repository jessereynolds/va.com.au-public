How to check and change email quotas at VA, on the qmail server:
by Jesse Reynolds, 23.10.01

First, log in to the mail host, polly.va.com.au, and su to root, then:

Checking a quota:

# vuserinfo <email_address>

eg: # vuserinfo jesse@va.com.au

Setting a quota:

# vsetuserquota <email_address> <size_in_bytes>

eg to set jesse's quota to 10Mb:

# vsetuserquota jesse@va.com.au 10000000

Note you should check the quota with vuserinfo after setting it to check that the change was successful.

Checking Disk Usage:

This is a little more complicated. What we do here is first find the directory that the user's email is stored in, then use the du command to find out how much data is in that directory, so:

# vuserinfo <email_address>

look in the output for the line "dir: ..." and copy the directory, then:

# du -sk <dir>

this will give you the usage in Kb.

eg:

bash-2.04# vuserinfo jesse@va.com.au
name: jesse
passwd: XUCzolxycDHLc
uid: 1
gid: 0
all services available
gecos: jesse reynolds
dir: |/usr/local/vpopmail/domains/va.com.au/jesse|
quota: 10000000

bash-2.04# du -sk /usr/local/vpopmail/domains/va.com.au/jesse
3917 /usr/local/vpopmail/domains/va.com.au/jesse
