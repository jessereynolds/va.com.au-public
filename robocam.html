The VA RoboCam (robocam.va.com.au) presented live video from Rundle Street in Adelaide, South Australia until Feb 2002 when it was relocated to the Fringe Hub at Adelaide University for the duration of the 2002 Adelaide Fringe. Virtual Artists commissioned the camera and have had it running since January 1996. The camera can be mounted outdoors and is fully controllable, including pan, tilt, zoom and focus.

Please visit the VA RoboCam for more details.
