ABOUT noise //

1. SO, WHAT IS NOISE?

noise is a festival event with a difference - one that celebrates, promotes and gives leverage to the work of young people in the arts, using the media as its canvas to exhibit a huge range of work from people aged 12-25 across the country.

An initiative that grew out of the embryo that was 1998's LOUD festival, noise facilitates outlets in many different facets of print, television, radio and online media to record and broadcast the attitudes and ideas of Australia's young artists, writers, musicians, filmmakers, designers, net heads and visionaries.

noise happened throughout the month of October 2001.

2. SO, WHAT WILL I FIND AT THIS SITE?

noise.net.au also hosts a good proportion of the amazing noise content - housing galleries, Flash animations, sounds, writing and more. Also, look around for feature stories, site highlights and other useful information.
