LOVE AND HATE: TELL US A TALE OR TWO 
There's a small hole developing in my trouser pocket, my left shoe wears out fractionally faster than my right one and the B string on my acoustic guitar has a funny twang that I can't get rid of. Now, while this might sound like a fascinating anecdote to some, it's not exactly the benchmark that you should be striving to equal when writing your submissions for noise's TRUE TALES OF LOVE AND HATE project. 
No. What we're after is interesting stories about your life, loves, hates, ups, downs, journeys or deepest, darkest thoughts - hell, you might even be able to find a way to make a story like mine sound worthy of reading if you feel passionately enough about such trivialities. But what we really want is the juicy stuff, like what gets you fired up, overjoyed with happiness, overwhelmed with rage or bedridden with misery.

TRUE TALES OF LOVE AND HATE is an interactive e-zine produced by noise that's open for submissions throughout October - we'll be publishing the best of them here on noise.net.au together with profiles of the talented sausages who created them.
But wait... in the well-respected words of Tim Shaw, there's more!

We're not just after your prose, poetry, or personal pining - you're also welcome to submit any imagery that's worthy of space on our visual gallery, as well as musical or audio creations of up to four minutes in length. Basically, as long as you are keeping within the themes of love, hate, relationships and the like, you can send us whatever you damn please.

To get those minds ticking over, here are some creatives you might want to work within:
- Short Fiction - up to 800 words of inspired creative writings
- Poetry - all sorts, any sort (prose, lyrics, free verse, blank verse, up-your-verse, narrative, plain ol' poetry)
- True Tales - personal, biographical, autobiographical writing...up to 1,500 words
- Visual gallery - digital images, illustrations, paintings, photographs, cartoons, comics...as long as it's visual
- Music Gallery - audio pieces/creations/tracks/bits...of up to 4 minutes.
- Don't Box Me In - for all creative creations that don't fit into the above categories...

Righto then, hop to it - TRUE TALES OF LOVE AND HATE submissions close Wednesday 31 October.

...and no running with scissors.
