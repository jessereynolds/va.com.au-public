POSTCARD DESIGNS // Earlier this year, noise and Avant Card invited budding young designers from around the country to create a postcard for the noise festival with only one specification - all designs were required to incorporate the web address of the noise site! The response to the project was fantastic. More than 1000 designs were received from designers aged from 12 to 25 years of age.
