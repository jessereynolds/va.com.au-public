HOW COMICS CAN CHANGE THE WORLD // We invited young women across Australia to send in autobiographical comic art that would go some way towards explaining how comics can change the world. The end result is a special collection of comics from more than a dozen artists each with a unique style and differing levels of experience.This project is a part of making noise, a joint initiative between noise and the Commonwealth government's Partnerships Against Domestic Violence initiative.

What's the deal with the covers?
One of the most complimented aspects of HOW COMICS CAN CHANGE THE WORLD was the amazing cover art. The comic book featured two different covers - superhero and indie-rock, drawn by Nancy Lorenz and Claire Robertson respectively.
