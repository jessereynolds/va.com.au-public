E-Works// Earlier this year, noise sent out an open-slather invitation for net heads to show what they do, from web layout, content and design to interactive and games. The result was a veritable goldmine of quality, where technology married thoughtful creativity and where young Australians took the publishing of their own ideas into their own hands. Click to the feature below to meet some of the artists and find out more about E-Works.
