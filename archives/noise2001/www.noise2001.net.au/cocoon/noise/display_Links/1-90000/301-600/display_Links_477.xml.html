NAVA Artist of the Day// Young visual artists were invited to step into the online arena when noise and The National Association for the Visual Arts devised the An Artist A Day project. As part of this initiative noise profiled a new visual artist every day during October - find out more about this tasty little project by taking a look-see at the below feature story, including sneak-peeks at the next generation of visual artists.
