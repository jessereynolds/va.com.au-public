TV HITS// One of the last projects to hit the festival turned out to be one of the broadest and the brightest. Together with teen mag TV Hits, noise discovered some new art being made by the young and media savvy. From fan art to experimental colour photography, the noise supplement showed just what's going on in the pop/art market. Find out more about the project by checking out the feature.
