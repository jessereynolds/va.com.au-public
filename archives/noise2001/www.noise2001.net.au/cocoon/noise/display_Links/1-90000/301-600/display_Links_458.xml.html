VOICEWORKS MAGAZINE // Voiceworks is a national quarterly magazine, published by Express Media, featuring writing and visual art by people under 25. The special noise edition of Voiceworks featured the work of young up-and-coming non-fiction writers who were asked to submit work that 'uses or abuses noise, as either a word or a concept'.
