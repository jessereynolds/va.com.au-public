ANTHOLOGY // Pluto Press and noise joined forces to produce an anthology of works created entirely by young Australians. Young creative types across the country were invited to send their words and pictures in to get published without begging. Within months more than 1000 submissions had been received from across the country and beyond. The end result is ANTHOLOGY...
