Monument// We didn't know what to expect, until faced with cutting hundreds of amazing interior, industrial, object, architectural and graphic design submissions down to just 20 pages for the Monument magazine noise supplement. Needless to say that as with all noise projects thus far they were all of an exceedingly high standard. Click on the feature below for more about the slick assortment of works, the artists and the project itself.
