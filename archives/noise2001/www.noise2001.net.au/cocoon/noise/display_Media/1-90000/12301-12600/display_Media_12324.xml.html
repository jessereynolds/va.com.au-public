name  // naturalight two 
creator  // Estelle Ihasz 
project  //NAVA ARTISTS 
I awoke each morning to a room filled with natural light...

I wanted to document this beauty. I was fascinated by the subtle changes in colour over time. My photography is about seeing, the way I intensely look at the world. It's about looking at the everyday with a different eye, new perspectives.

Anything can be beautiful, it's just the way you perceive it.


See more of Estelle's work, read more about her and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Estelle is the Artist of the Day, 11/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
