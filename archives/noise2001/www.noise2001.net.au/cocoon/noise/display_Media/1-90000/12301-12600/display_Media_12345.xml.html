name  // Blanket and Chair 
creator  // Ben Hosking 
project  //NAVA ARTISTS 
I like filling the frame of my shots with the subject. Trying to capture the form of a subject, perhaps stumbling across repeating patterns in natural and man-made objects or making the normal appear different. The shapes and shadow play that caught my eye here demanded that I stop whatever it was that I was doing and make sure I tried to cature the image that caught my eye and put it onto film.


Check out more of Ben's work, read more about him and meet a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Ben is the Artist of the Day, 9/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
