name  // Matthew Kowald, 'Future of Der Funk'. 
creator  // Matthew Kowald 
project  //ABC TV 
'Future of Der Funk' is a short action sequence about a guy being chased through four worlds.
Originally made as the 'opener' for the Queensland University of Technology Showcase for final year film students, it is an explosive exploration of live action through different aesthetics.

"I wanted to create something that experimented with as many camera and post-production techniques as possible. It was a crazy shoot - we ended up taking the camera underwater during a thunderstorm, strapping it to a stunt motorcycle and even driving it across a desert-like location 10 ks from the Brisbane CBD. We had so little film on this shoot that we actually ran out on the last shot."


Matthew's work features in the Saturday 20th October edition of ABC's noise tv, 10.30 am - 11.30 am, repeated Saturday night before 'Rage'.

'Future of Der Funk' is also featured during ABC TV's Short Fuse program, a 'best of' short films and animations submitted to noise. Every Friday night during October from 11.00 pm to 11.30 pm on ABC TV.

Check out abc.net.au/noise for more information about ABC/noise projects.
