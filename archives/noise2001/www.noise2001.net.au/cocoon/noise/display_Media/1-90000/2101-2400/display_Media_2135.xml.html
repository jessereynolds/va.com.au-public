DIGITAL MIXED MEDIA

This work arose from my experimentation with contrasting figures and locations, lifting a style and medium (in this case, expressionist paint) and placing it within the setting of a wholly different style and medium (photorealism). The result is a work that attempts to transcend time and space through use of technology.
