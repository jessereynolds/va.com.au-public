name  // 'Jasper' - conceptual chair | online animation 
creator  // Paolo de Jesus 
project  //FLASH ANIMATION 
'Jasper' is an animation that explores chair design and the nature of design in general.

It looks at themes of virtual worlds and how this relates to: the design process, design as mental activity, simulacra, designed objects as media, designer heroes, good design, style; modernism/post-modernism, fashion and society.
