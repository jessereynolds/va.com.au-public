This is a detail from a small acrylic painting on canvas called "Dreamday"

Born in Western Australia, I took up art at a young age and knew that it was an area of life that I was most interested in, at the age of 19 I completed a certificate in Art and Design and continued on to the Western Australian School of Art and Design, whilst there I completed a Diploma in Graphic Design an specialised in Multimedia. I've been working professionally for over two years in the multimedia industry in Perth and Melbourne and use my traditional fine art skills as a sanctuary from the corporate world of design. I have recently completed a series of paintings and drawings which I'm eager to exhibit.


See more of Jamie's work, read about him, and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.
