project  //NAVA ARTISTS 
Anna is inspired by people - what makes them tick, what gets them going, why they do what they do - and what it means to them.

In the final year of a Bachelor of Fine Arts, Anna lives in Ballarat and is working on a piece based on humans and the information they need.


Read more about Anna, see some of her work (this image illustrates the project and is not one of Anna's) and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.
