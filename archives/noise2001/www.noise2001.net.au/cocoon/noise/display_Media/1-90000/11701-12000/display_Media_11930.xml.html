...an image of a red tomato on a red background.

I have a vivid imagination, but I can't imagine not being a photographer.

I'm a photography assistant at the Uni of Southern QLD and my job allows me the flexibility to do my own photography after work hours. It's a great place to learn and I'm updating my folio at the moment for an exhibition in Toowoomba next year. I'm hoping to move on soon, into another position so I can keep learning, and I hope one day to have my own studio where I can inflict my unique style of photography onto the public!


See more of Sarah's work, read her artist's statement, and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists on the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.
