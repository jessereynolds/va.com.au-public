I wanted to create a Super Hero alter ego and she needed somewhere to live.

This is why I created this site. It was one of those "cannot-bear-to-watch-any-more-day-time-television" moments during the holidays when I thought an alter ego might make my day a bit more interesting.

My site is under constant renovation. Each time I learn a new program or technique I try to incorporate it into the site. I like using scanned images and there are a lot of doodles from my uni notebooks (which lack lecture notes, surprisingly) that I have scanned and animated. I'm trying to cut the renovations down to once a month and do some work on the content, but I'm easily distracted.
