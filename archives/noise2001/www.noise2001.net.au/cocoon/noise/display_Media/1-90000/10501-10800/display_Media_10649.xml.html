These images are of myself as different characters.

They are taken from my recent video "And I Cried", where I played every character in the piece.

I have a comic called "RelaxBaby" which I self publish, which is up to its 4th issue. I also make (mostly) screen based experimental videos, which play around Melbourne, and my most recent video "And I Cried" is part of a travelling festival in the U.S.A. I am studying my Masters at R.M.I.T., specialising in making video pieces.

With my comics I write up notes for the layout first, work out how many panels I need, do it all in pencil first, and then trace over the pencil. Definately need an eraser. Videos take so much longer. I have no idea how I do it.

My influences are Aline Kominsky Crumb, George Kuchar, Crispin Glover, trailers for films, soap operas, danoz direct, rock'n roll.

If you want to see more of my stuff you can email me at ctytler@netspace.net.au My comics sell in Polyester Books in Melbourne. My video "And I Cried" is playing at the "Wet and Dry" festival in September at the Treasury Theatre in Melb. I also sell copies of it at Polyester Books.


Cassandra will be appearing on ABC's noise tv on Saturday morning 20th October, 10.30 am - 11.30 am, repeated Saturday night before 'Rage'.

Check out abc.net.au/noise for more information about ABC/noise projects.
