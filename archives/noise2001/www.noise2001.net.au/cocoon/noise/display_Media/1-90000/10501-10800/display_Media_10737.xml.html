Kate Lau - 23 year old Glass artist from Canberra:

"I love the way that light shines through transparent glass. You get beautiful reflections on tables and things like that where the light is shining through the glass and the colour and the texture and the patterns are being reflected onto other surfaces."


Kate Lau's profile will appear on Triple J, Radio National, News Radio and your local ABC Radio station throughout October.
