"Michael Dransfield, my first poetic hero. This poem translates the only conversation that I have ever had with him. And may never have again."

--

WHITE WASH DREAMING

dreamt of Dransfield
last night
went to visit him
in M ward

distracted him enough
with talk
of exhuming language
for him to talk

sifting insight
through madness
tried to convince him
to come back

but the nurse
told me visiting hour
was over
had to go

apologised
if I'd freaked him out
coming from the future
and all

nah man
he said
you don't understand
that's cool


For Michael Dransfield, 1948-1973,
a poet too early for this world.
