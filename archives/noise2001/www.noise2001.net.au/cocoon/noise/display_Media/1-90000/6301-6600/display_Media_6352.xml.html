name  // Debt Pops 
creator  // Jody Ekert 
project  //FAKE ADS 
We live in a society of "buy now - think later".

Credit is offered as a quick, attractive fix, and
barely anyone escapes their teens and twenties without debt. "Debt Pops" attempts to market debt as it really is - something easily purchased and nice tasting but ultimately bad for you - especially if you don't read the small print.
