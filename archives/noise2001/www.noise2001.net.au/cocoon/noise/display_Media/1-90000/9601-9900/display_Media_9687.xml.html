Yes there really is Music in the Sticks.

Music In The Sticks is a FReeZA funded youth committee, which have been successfully running music and other drug and alcohol free events in the Macedon Ranges Shire for the past four years. MITS has a membership of approximately thirty young people (ranging in age from 11 to 21), who plan, organize and run events.


Music In The Sticks will be featured on ABC's noise tv on Saturday morning 27th October, 10.30 am - 11.30 am, repeated Saturday night before 'Rage'.

Check out abc.net.au/noise for more information about ABC/noise projects
