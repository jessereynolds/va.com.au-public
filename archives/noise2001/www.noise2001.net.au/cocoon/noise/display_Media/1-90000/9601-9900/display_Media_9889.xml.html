Anna Seedhouse - 20 year old writer from Townsville, Queensland:

"I take notebooks with me everywhere I go, and if anything interesting or amusing happens to me, I jot it down. In a couple of months, if I still find it interesting or particularly funny, I'll expand it out into a story or use it in something else I'm writing at the time."
