Gallon O Gravy are Daniel Kuseta and Anthony Mays from Victoria.

They have been working together since 1996. They have had ongoing programs on 3MU (Monash University) and SYN FM (RMIT) in and have worked with RMIT.

They are plugging away at their movie script in the time left over from avoiding jury duty.


Gallon O' Gravy's work features in the Saturday 6th October late edition of ABC's noise tv, before 'Rage'.

Check out abc.net.au/noise for more information about ABC/noise projects.
