name  // Curtain Series 3/5 
creator  // Estelle Ihasz 
project  //ONLINE GALLERY 
This series of work began with two images taken within a hotel room in Melbourne...

There was some kind of magic in those curtains, the play of the light and the breeze which captured my attention. These curtains sparked a whole new body of work. I began recording the light streaming through my curtains at home, capturing the essence of my seeing. I believe my images work on another level, they speak a lot about myself, who I am.

There is a sense of journey, the viewer is able to enter into my images and see beneath my eyes.
