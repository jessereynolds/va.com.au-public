beecroft 2119
by eugene chew
november 1999

beecroft boasts the highest spending in the nation
on women's hairdressing
with five fancy salons in our small row of shops
alone
I walk past them on my way to the railway station
well usually running because I can see
the wires twanging spastically against the sky
as my train
unseen and unusually on time
rounds the corner from pennant hills
obscured by the line of camphor laurels
that tee off our small row of shops like a pair of scissors
rounding the square edge of a woman's fringe
