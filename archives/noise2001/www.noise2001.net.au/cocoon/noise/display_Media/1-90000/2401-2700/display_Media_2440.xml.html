name  // Avenging Underwear (and other burning issues) 
creator  // John Bergin 
project  //JUICE MAGAZINE 
It occurred to me the other day...
that everything is so politically correct in today's world that one false move has the potential to incite moral outrage and frenzied media debate.

So, I chose to lampoon this fact by selecting the most mundane, everyday item I could lay my hands on - it just so happens I hadn't done my laundry that day - and illustrating it as some sort of sizzling, topical material frozen in time moments before descending upon an unsuspecting populace, causing widespread public division and fuelling dinner-party conversation for months to come.
