name  // R U A STUPID FUCKER 
creator  // Christine Polowyj 
project  //ONLINE GALLERY 
This is the third and final pic in a series of three experimental digital illustrations. The entire series is titled "Fashion Gone Wrong". The works were all created on my computer as 400dpi/CMYK files in Photoshop 6.0. The fills in this entire image originate from rubbish i have found on the ground (and office bins at my cleaning job) in Brisbane and Melbourne.Dimensions of R U A STUPID FUCKER is: 50cm x 60cm. Due to the size of the image (the flattened file is over 390Mb), i divided the work into 17 vertical sections. I had no idea what the pieced-together image would look like until after all the work was done. Thankfully, my experiment turned out well!
