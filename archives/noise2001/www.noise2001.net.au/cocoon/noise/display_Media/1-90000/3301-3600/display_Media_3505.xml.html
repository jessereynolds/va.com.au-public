creator  // Millie Fairhall 
project  //ONLINE GALLERY 
Newspaper, ink, pastel, bit of dad's masonite from the shed, whiteout... that's all!!

Coming towards the end of grade 12 you're like stuffed with all this "future" grow-up" "get a stable job" rah rah blah stuff and I guess this represents this... we're forced to pursue things which will make us money... let us have the huge house picket fence 2 kids and the golden retriever sitting at the front gate ready to bowl the oh so perfect chiselled jaw husband over when he gets home from his stable office job... lalalalala... that's the ideal life set out for us right?! Oh crap... sorry I'm not heading in that direction... following my heart... doing what I want... that's the only success I'll ever get out of life... sorry if that's not what you had planned out for me... "There are many things in life that will catch your eye, but only a few will capture your heart... pursue those"
