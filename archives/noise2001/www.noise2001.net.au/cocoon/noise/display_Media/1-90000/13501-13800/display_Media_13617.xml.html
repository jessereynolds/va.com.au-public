A piece of poetry I wrote about a very good friend of mine.

--

AN ODE TO THOSE WHO LOVE


Those who love,
Those rare few who see with eyes unclouded,
Not blinded in the fog of Pride, Deceit or Doubt.
Those who love without condition,
Those who love while still alone.

Those who love,
Those rare few who reach out with hands unblemished,
Not soiled by the mud of Fear, Hate or Greed,
Those who love with their actions,
Those who love while still in pain.

Those who love,
Those rare few who speak with tongues untarnished,
Not laced with the venom of Bitterness, Dishonesty or Spite,
Those who love without resent.
Those who love while still not knowing the gift they give.

Those who love,
Those rare few who regard even a monster with compassion.
Those who can look past his ugliness
and see the loneliness in his eyes,
and the pain in his heart,
Those who can treat him as someone special.

Only those who love,
Those rare few, only those who love can do that.

Their presence is light to those in shadow,
Their touch is warmth to those who are cold,
Their words are soothing waters to those in pain,
Their love is sustinence to those who are starving.

Those who love......

Those rare few who make life worth living.


They are the loveliest of them all.



God bless those who love.
