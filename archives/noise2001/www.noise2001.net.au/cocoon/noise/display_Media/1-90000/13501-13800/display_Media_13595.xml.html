project  //TRUE TALES OF LOVE AND HATE 
A tribute to the love, respect and awe that exists between privileged siblings.

OF YOUR BLOOD AND HEART

Of Your Blood and Heart

Under fluorescent neon light
He still provides the glare.
When the rest of us are blind
He perceives a shadow flicker.
Amidst all ferociousness that
Deafens from mum's room,
His composure still shines pure.

When I'm struggling to catch my
Tears, he brings me to a river
So I can see they're not alone.
After all his disappointments
His heart still holds me up.
When I shiver at what's in dad's eyes
He still knows to clench his teeth
And steady mine as well.

Even now away, now standing blurred
In my mind, you
Let me push off from you.
You push me with a breath.
You push me with a whisper.
You push me so you let me believe
I'm doing this on my own.
