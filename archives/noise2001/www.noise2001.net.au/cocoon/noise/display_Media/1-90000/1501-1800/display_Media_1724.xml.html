name  // Star 69 (fatty acid remix) 
creator  // Julian Ramundi 
project  //FATBOY SLIM REMIX 
This was put together rather quickly, on the last day of submissions for the contest. I took one of the beats of the song and added my own quirky melody to it. I didn't use too many of the vocal samples, as I wanted to do something a bit different and unique to the track, as most people would probably have used the vocals as the forefront of their remix. It was done on Acid software, hence the name. I's not as long as I would have liked it to be, but it gets my idea across rather well. I hope you enjoy it!
