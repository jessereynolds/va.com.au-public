project  //SBS 
Media students at Macquarie University, Sydney were asked to produce an audio feature.

The piece was to include a variety of voices, music and sound effects. The only requirement was that the students must use the words NOISE or JUSTICE as inspiration for their work.

A larger selection of these works will be available for download on the SBS Whatever website during the month of October.


Media student Stephen speaks about the audio piece he co-produced...

No real inspiration for this piece, just seemed like a good idea that hadn't really been explored to my knowledge - though it probably has!

I achieved the result I after, as it was a light piece that was quite funny.
