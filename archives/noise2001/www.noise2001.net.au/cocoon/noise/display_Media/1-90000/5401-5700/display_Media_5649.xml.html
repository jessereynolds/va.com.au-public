name  // The Nerve Game 
creator  // Van Sowerwine 
project  //FLASH ANIMATION 
You're running late for work. You've got too much on.

You're stressed and tense and you desperately dodge your way through friends, bosses and parties in a frantic attempt to hold your life together.

See your stress and depression rise to levels never seen before! Watch yourself collapse then explode! Explore the part of your life you&radicd rather forget about!

I've always been a stress head and I was going through a bad time where I felt like I had way too much to do and not enough time. So I decided to make a game to reflect that. I used a comic style to try and create a very personal feeling throughout the game, especially when the person dies.

I basically drew a walk cycle - the person walking - then superimposed backgrounds and put in stressed screams. I asked a sound artist, Cornel Wiltzek, to do a soundtrack that had an eighties computer game feel and he came up with the music which I love. For a while I got addicted to it and couldn't get it out of my head (you have been warned).
