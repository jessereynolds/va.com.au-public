name  // Goodnight Beautiful 
creator  // Lachlan Mackay 
project  //TRUE TALES OF LOVE AND HATE 
My Grandma and Grandpa have been married for 63 years, and their love for each other that has survived through good and bad times has inspired me to write this poem. One time while I was staying with my grandparents, grandpa before going to bed said to grandma 'Goodnight Beautiful' and this really touched me. This is what i think love and marriage is all about - the fact that at 85, they still love, respect and care for each other.

--

He's 85 years old
And She is too.
62 years of marriage,
That's quite a few.

He was once a working-class man,
A highly skilled fitter and turner.
Five children he raised.
He was the breadwinner and the earner.

After all these years,
After all this time,
Goodnight beautiful
Is still his closing line.

With God to guide them
And God to lead,
Their life is a book
That we all should read.

He's now nearly blind
And practically deaf.
Skills of the past forgotten,
She's all that is left.

He may be a bit gruff,
And he is slowing down,
But goodnight beautiful
Is still His solid ground.

When I'm 85
And my wife is too,
I know what it is
That I want to do;

After God's lead my life,
Utter those words that last forever;
Goodnight beautiful,
I'm glad we've spent all those years together.
