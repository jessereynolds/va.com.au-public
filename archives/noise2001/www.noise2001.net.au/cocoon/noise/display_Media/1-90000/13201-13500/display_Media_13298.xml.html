"While I swing I have permission to think about nothing and everything...
Soothed into a lovely dreamy state by the rhythm of the swing I get caught up in the job of being every age - all at once."

'Swing' is a visual essay about the awkward joy of remembering, and attempting to absorb, every story, every place, every experience.

'Swing' is the second short Beth has written and directed. She recently completed a Bachelor of Media at Macquarie University. Her dream is to make documentaries which celebrate the minutiae of obsession. As training she herself has spent her life constantly obsessing over one thing and another.


'Swing' featured during ABC TV's Short Fuse program, a 'best of' short films and animations submitted to noise. Every Friday night during October from 11.00 pm to 11.30 pm on ABC TV.

Check out abc.net.au/noise for more information about ABC/noise projects.
