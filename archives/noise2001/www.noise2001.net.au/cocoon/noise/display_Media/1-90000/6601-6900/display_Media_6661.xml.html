name  // The Day Dad Flooded the House 
creator  // Desiree Cross 
project  //FLASH ANIMATION 
The amusing true story of when Dad's late night bath had disasterous consequences.

When I was told at Uni to make a 3-minute cartoon/movie on the theme of water I couldn't resist telling the true story of when Dad got smashed and flooded the house. I'm really lucky that I've got him to provide me with outrageous material. I don't have to labor over writing new scripts. I just ring Mum up and say, 'What's he done now?' Eventually I'd like to make more cartoons based on these characters.

I was really disorganised when making the flash. I'd drawn some rough pictures on the computer and had an idea of how I wanted to tell the story, but wasn't sure how to get it out. It wasn't until I got my brother Luke into the sound recording booth to do the voices that the story came alive. Normally I'd get the script as perfect as I could before even starting to sketch some art, but this time I sort of let it happen as it happened. I don't recommend this method. It ended up taking me three times as long to get it finished because I kept changing my mind. I learnt my lesson though.
