This was my submission for a t'shirt design comp. held by 183 Productions in Qld. I think it was one of only about three entries received!! Anyway, nothing came of it, yet I couldn't let it go to waste so here it is.
The map of Aus was Streamlined from a photo I set up and shot in my backyard with a crap load of cans. I don't think you can spot it here but every single can along the front edge is different! Oooh! The text was written with an old chisel tip marker, scanned in and streamlined. The whole thing was composed in Illustrator. Aaaaah Vector!!
