name  // Pepe's Quest 
creator  // Zeldz Magnoonis 
project  //ESKY MAGAZINE 
Every generation, the heavens deliver 11 marked children
to the ball hunting tribes of Mercezille, every one a leap in evolution.

The 10th of these (Pepe), was prophesised to posess the greatest potential of all! Upon birth however, Pepe's DNA was cloned by the evil Victor Vangreubles. He used it to create a superior team with which he could take control of the Valley League.

To restore order, Mercezille elders challenged Vangreubles to a match in one years time, the loser to be forever banished to the relegation plains. At the same time, they sent Pepe on his rites of passage quest to gain knowledge and experience, allowing him to fulfill the no. 10 legacy, a true lord of Mercezille.

This is his tale...
