name  // 2/3 - 'Move' Office 
creator  // John Ellway 
project  //MONUMENT MAGAZINE 
Object Description // The Brief for this design concept was to create a single piece or range of office furniture products that fit the criteria of being commercially acceptable. Design considerations included the client's manufacturing facilities and materials; Ease of assembly and; the Development of existing shapes and components.

In answering the brief, the focus in this design has been to create a modular range of products that allow the owner or occupier a sense of personalisation, yet practical enough that the environment can be used by more than one person. This has been achieved through the inclusion multiple retractable privacy panels on the desk screening system, which allow the occupier to attach personal items to their panel. The design also features a circular shelving unit on castors. Perhaps each occupier of the workspace has their own shelving unit they can wheel around? Each level of the shelving unit is surrounded in a semi-transparent fabric, such as sail cloth and is with velcro to the the edge of the circular shelf. The top shelf features a glowing lamp.
