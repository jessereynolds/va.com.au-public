blurb  // We are trapeze artists and circus performers.

Bonnie Blewitt:
I have been a member of the Southern Edge Arts circus workshops for the past 12 months. Over this time I have trained very hard in many acts, including double trapeze, lera, acro-balance, dance, contortion, three girls and static trapeze.

My love of circus is growing and I hope to develop a career out of my training at Southern Edge Arts, as a professional trapeze artist or stunt woman. I've previously done 6 years of Gymnastics and I'm a pretty good acrobat. I decided to try circus as it has more variety and different acts to learn and perform.

Crystal Warner:
I have been at Southern Edge Arts for the past six years, and have worked on many acts, including Swinging trapeze, three girls trapeze, web, juggling, uni-cycling, tight rope, contortion, vaulting/mini tramp, acrobatics, balances, static trapeze, Double trapeze and dance.

While in South Africa I worked on Swinging and Three girls trapeze developing and learning the right technique to improve our routines so they will flow more gracefully and be safer.

I had four years of Gymnastics training before leaving Perth and moving to Albany where I joined Southern Edge Arts to try out circus. After one turn on the trapeze I was hooked and have worked on many different styles of aerial work since then.

I love being part of a circus school as it is a great team effort to create a show and is awesome fun to be part of a performance that you know you have helped create.
