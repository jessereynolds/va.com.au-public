blurb  // I am a Graphic Design Student at the University of Canberra.


I started writing electronic music in 1995 when I discovered Scream Tracker 3 and an 8bit Soundblaster card (I was 15.) I copped a flogging at school as my friends loathed my passion for 'techno' :) nothing was going to change me.

Over the past few years with 2 friends (Nick Langdale-Smith and Bodhi Philpot) I have built up a music studio. Togther we have lots of toys, samplers, synths, and soundcards. We write music under the name tCR (Third Channel Research) and love writing music and finding phat sounds, generated electronically or from prerecorded samples.

We've thought about playing live though haven't really had enough practice or developed the appropriate techniques. We believe playing live means creating your own sounds live, thus requires alot of skill, more so than playing prerecorded music. We have many contacts in the industry so getting a gig shouldn't be to difficult if it eventuates.

I run a company called Ridge id Logik, which offers services such as laser shows, computer consultancy and audio hire.
