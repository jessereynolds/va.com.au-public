blurb  // I like to create strange flash animations that are vague and not always to the point.

If you look hard enough, and on the right angle, it's possible to see where I was coming from. I also sketch and paint a little, but ever since I found flash in June 2000, I haven't looked back.
.::.
I've always loved watching cartoons. When I used to watch Transformers and the Wuzzles, I knew what I wanted to do. After years of basic sketching I discovered Flash Animation. It re-kindled my passion for cartoons, and I imediately began making my debut cartoons.
.::.
I make my cartoons using Macromedia Flash.
Before making my cartoons I need to have a clear idea in my mind before I start... actually, I usually have no idea when I begin. I draw all my images on paper, scan them into my PC, and then colour and label each part in Flash. Then I record all the voices for the cartoon, and animate. I leave the Lip-synching until last, because I HATE it. After I finish each cartoon I upload it onto my site for you good people to ridicule.
.::.
I love everything about flash, but it's sometimes frustrating when I have an idea that I don't have the imediate skills to accomplish. I'd love to have a team to work with, and churn out 3 or 4 cartoons a week.
.::.
I am a 23 year old who was born in Alice Springs, I now live in Victoria. Not much more to tell here.
.::.
My Inspirations: NEWGROUNDS.COM, HR.GIGER, CW. STONEKING, TIM BURTON, JEFF BUCKLEY, ANIME, Pi, CATCH 22, KURT VONNEGUT.
.::.
People can see my artwork, animations and music at: http://www.thistler.com

animations also showing on Newgrounds.com and Fandomfilms.com.
.::.
