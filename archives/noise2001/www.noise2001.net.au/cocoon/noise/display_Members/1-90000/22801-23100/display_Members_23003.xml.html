blurb  // Take cynicism and add water; instant me.

Once upon a time I would never have imagined trying to put themes or messages into any of my screenwriting or film work, I would simply try to entertain. But as I've grown older and as I've developed thoughts and theories about the world we live in, filmmaking has become a way of getting some sort of truth out into the world. Art always tells the truth, even when it's lying. Isn't that from a movie somewhere?

My work is often comic and dark, and the way the two moods play off each other is one of my favorite things about what I do. I like to think I'm further along than I am, but we can all learn, even me. My writing hints at Tarantino, my film work hints at Kubrick and Scorsese and Coppola. Most of my stuff can be seen at www.esotericrabbit.cjb.net
and I hope to have more stuff playing in more places soon. Which is good.

I love film. I love the way it can make someone feel something, the way it can show or depict or subject or object or enthrall or entertain, or all at once. Its versatility. It's addictive.

It's an addiction; like with heroin, the only cure for film, is more film. Simple as that.
