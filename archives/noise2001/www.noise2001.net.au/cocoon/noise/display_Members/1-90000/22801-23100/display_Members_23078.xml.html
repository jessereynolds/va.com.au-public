blurb  // A Young, creative, energetic, dedicated Producer on the move... Have you heard of him? If not you soon will... His name is Shane Cunningham!

I grew up in the country, South Australia and started in the film industry when I was 15 years old. Over the past four years I've made made some crucial contacts and met some really interesting people.

I'm currently in discussion with Sony Music regarding a Killing Heidi project. The project has been passed through Wah Wah Music (band management) and now awaiting Sony's official approval.

I'm also involved in the development of the Australian feature film Revelations that's currently financed and securing worldwide distribution.

Huge things are forecast for Revelations and you're sure to hear a lot more during 2003-4.

I've worked on features, shorts, docos, music clips and studied film and television in Adelaide during 2000. I completed year 11 in my small hometown, and then re-located to the city to further my career. I began studying, and soon started developing my own projects.

I live for Producing in the world's film industry. I find it an exciting time in Australia at present, with international production companies shooting in Australia it's giving our talent and crew the opportunities in our home country. This is just the beginning...

I'm currently 19 years of age and have been living in Sydney for the past five months. My main goal was to begin my career as young as possible, this has been something I have been working on for some time. I've previously discussed projects with John Watson (Silverchair's manager), Wah Wah Music (Killing Heidi), The Gersh Agency (Los Angeles) and many US production companies, the South Australian Film Corporation, Australian Film Commission, Sony Music Entertainment, ABC, Triple J, plus lots more. I'm on my way to fulfilling my goals...

During early 2003 I will be re-locating to Melbourne for pre-production for 'Revelations' and hopefully have a few other projects in the works, who knows where this will lead me, I just enjoy it, it's a ride... And it's only the beginning...
