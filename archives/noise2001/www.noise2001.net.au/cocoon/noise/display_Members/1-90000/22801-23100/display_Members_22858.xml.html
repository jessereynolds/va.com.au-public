blurb  // Tell us about yourself...
I moved to Australia 5 years ago after growing up
in England where I studied fine art, history of art and design technology at A-level. I used to be a tennis coach and rugby union jock before the australian heat and lifestyle affected my brain -I'm now a visual artist, a musician and a drunk. I completed my Bachelor of Visual Arts in Fine Art at QCA in 2000 majoring in Intermedia. I am now working on my Masters in Communication Design at QUT.

Exactly what do you do?..
I concentrate mainly on digital media for my artwork, I've been playing with interactive pieces recently - I love photoshop, flash and premiere. The reaction a good piece of art or music can give is very satisfying.

Why do you do what you do??..
I have a lot of fun, isn't that what life supposed to be about?. I love being creative and expressing myself throughmusic and art.

How do you do it?...
Well it's a tough job but someone has to do it. I could never see myself in a suit at a desk making money for someone else.

What do you like/ dislike about your work?
I love the permanancy of it - that someone could be looking at or even playing with my work long after I'm gone. I hate the financial constraints -I wonder what I'd be producing if I was a millionaire?.

What/ who are you influences?
My Family & Friends, Bacon, Shiele, Sherman, Warhol, Alchohol, Foster, Rodgers, Lloyd-Wright, Queens of the Stoneage, Scrabble, the weather, B.R.M.C, Connoly, Izzard, The League of Gentlemen, Mammoth Volume, Erwin Olaf, Vicious Hairy Mary, vicissitude, Budd, money, nature, technology, Ginger and strangely shaped fruit.
