blurb  // Artist of the day, 7/10/01.

art design digital techno painting SuperFlat...

I am a 3rd year painting student at the Canberra School of Art. I make designs, paintings and animation using photoshop, illustrator and flash.

I'm inspired by the work of visual artists like Ricky Swallow, Takashi Murakami, Richard Hamilton, Stephen Bram and Vivienne Binns, as well as Techno from Detroit (Underground Resistance, Plastikman, Jeff Mills) and Anime esp. Macross Plus, Ghost in the Shell etc etc etc. in the future i would like to travel some more. I aim to be a practising artist + designer.


See more of Michael's work, read more about him and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Michael is the Artist of the Day, 7/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
