blurb  // My name is Millie and I'm an artsy chicka among other things!!

I guess the main thing I do is paint. I started painting in Grade 10, but have pretty much been into art my whole life! I love artsy-ing because for me it's freedom.

"Painting is freedom. If you jump you might fall on the other side of the rope. But if you're not willing to take the risk of breaking your neck what good is it? You don't jump at all. You have to wake people up. To revolutionise their way of identifying things. You've got to create images they won't accept. Force them to understand that they're living in a pretty queer world. A world that's not what they think it is."

This is a quote from Picasso and when I paint I try to do this. There's always going to be elements of painting you can't lose, but you've got to do your own thing.

Likes and dislikes about my work... I can't say that!! It's too weird for me. I either like my work or totally palm it off as... not likeable!!! There are no in-betweens I guess.

Influences... every person and everything that hits my life influences me and my art. From a guy who's waiting out the back for the sets to come through whilst surfing, to my own friends or family. They all influence me in some way no matter if it's positive or negative, but most of the time originality occurs.

The main person that influences my art is Picasso along with artist/surfer Oska Wright. These two guys influence me because their art and general persona have changed the way the public views the world, which I admire and hope to do someday myself.

Surfing is another thing which influences me. I love surfing because it to is freedom and fun. It has given me so much insight into both life and myself. It has led me to be in some of the most beautiful places in the world, meet some of the most extraordinary people and experience some pretty crazy things, all of which influence my art.

Art is freedom. Have fun with it and do what you want with it.
