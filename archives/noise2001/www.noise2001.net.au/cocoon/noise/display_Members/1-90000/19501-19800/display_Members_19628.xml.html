blurb  // Taurus, Snake, ABC ... film, graphics, web, that's me.

Exactly what is it that you do?

I'm in my seventh year at UNSW, 2 months away from completing a BA(Hons)/BCom. I'm also working as a web producer for Real Networks, and writing and filmmaking in my spare time.

Why do you do what you do?
Because I feel life slipping by, taking many beautiful things with it. I'm very sentimental. I try to capture a few of these little moments to remind myself and others, of feelings that deserved to linger a little longer.

How do you do it?
Long periods of waiting and hoping for something or someone to provide that vital kick of inspiration... every now and then self-disgust hits first and I push myself to start a new project... things tend to snowball from there.

What do you like/dislike about your work?
It's underdeveloped and a bit amateurish. On the other hand, they're very personal and sincere creations. This gives them an intertextual quality that builds relationships with the viewer.

Tell us about yourself - who are you?
A 23 year old azn-aussie with dreams of being a filmmaker... I grew up in Melbourne and Sydney, with regular family hols in Malaysia. Started doing zines in high school, then moved onto student newspapers and short films. In the last few years, I've travelled a lot and worked in various industries, exercising my curiosity and fascination with different cultures.

What/who are your influences?
A few names amongst many: Bukowski, Kundera, Calvino, Capote, Sony, Apple, Godard et al, Imamura, Hartley, Coen bros, Wong Kar Wai, Tsai Ming Liang, Jeffrey Smart, Tortoise, Ge Lan, Talib Kweli, Shena Ringo... anything that speaks with sincerity, humour and attack. And of course my friends who make everything seem possible and exciting

How can people see more of your stuff?
Got a personal website in the works, and may be getting screened at this year's Sydney Asia-Pacific Film Festival. They could also check out toto... an online film mag I set up with a friend.
