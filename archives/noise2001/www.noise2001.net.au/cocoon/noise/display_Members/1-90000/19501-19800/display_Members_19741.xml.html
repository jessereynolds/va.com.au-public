blurb  // James Stuart is a poet, but also an author of fiction and non-fiction.

After selling his soul, he received the 2000 Harri Jones Memorial Prize, awarded annually to an Australian poet under the age of 35. He was a nude panellist at the 2000 National Young Writers Festival and is a forgotten ex-editor of the University of Newcastle student newspaper, Opus.

James also DJs under the alias of Neotoke at dodgy parties and dingy clubs in Newcastle, and in Sydney, where he now resides. He is in love with a girl currently on the other side of the Timor Sea.
