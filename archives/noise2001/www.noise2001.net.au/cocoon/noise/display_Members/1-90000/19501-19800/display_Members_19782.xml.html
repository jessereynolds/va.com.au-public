blurb  // Artist of the day, 6/10/01

I'm a photographer at the University of Southern Queensland. I love creative and artistic photography - I like making real things look unreal. I know this will sound boring but photographing flowers is what I wish I could do all day - just me, my camera and a bunch of flowers....aaahhh...
I've got the worst camera in the world - it's a piece of junk - but it makes it harder to get a precise image, so I have to work harder and that's a good thing.
It's a tough industry, photography, I wonder how I'll go in the future....
