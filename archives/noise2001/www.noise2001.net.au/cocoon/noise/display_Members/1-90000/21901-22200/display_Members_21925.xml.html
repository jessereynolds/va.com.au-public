blurb  // Artist of the Day, 9/10/01.

I took up photography about a year ago after wanting to for quite some time. I grew up always wishing I had a mini camera inside my eye so I could capture all the wonderful images that came across my sight: landscapes, repeating natural and man-made patterns, people's facial expressions and body language.

I have been lucky to come from a creative family, and have myself a number of creative interests including; writing (see pieces of me), photography (see ben hosking studio), music journalism (see 27twelve.8m.com), writing and performing music (see dracosucks and red-solutionsin sound) and web design. Unfortunately I love them all, which keeps me perpetually poor!

I have an interest in many of the fields of photography including Documentary photography, Automotive photography (still life), Architectural photography and Landscape photography. I aspire to some day reach the level of expertise of artists such as David Perry, Jeff Carter, Richard Avedon and Alfred Eisenstaedt to name a few.


See some of Ben's work (the image displayed represents our project and is not one of Ben's), read more about him and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Ben is the Artist of the Day, 9/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
