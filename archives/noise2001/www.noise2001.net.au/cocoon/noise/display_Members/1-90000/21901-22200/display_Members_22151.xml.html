blurb  // Artist of the day, 21/10/01

I am an artist / writer / researcher currently living in Brisbane and undertaking a Masters of Arts (Research). My practice encounters aspects of installation, photography, performance, and video. Striving to be financially sustainable as a professional artist, I also dabble at times as a writer, curator and arts administrator. At the completion of my Masters I hope to relocate to Berlin or London.

I have great respect for the work of Dennis Del Favero, Felix Gonzales - Torres, Nan Goldin, Larry Clark, Chris Cunningham, Rineke Dijkstra, and countless lesser-known emerging artists in Brisbane, Sydney and Melbourne.

See some of Jose's work, read more about him and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Jose is the Artist of the Day, 21/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
