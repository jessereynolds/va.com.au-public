blurb  // I like total control over my work hence the title for some of my submitted works.

I basically love to create enviroments/ narratives from ideas I have conjured up and which are essentially a reflection of what I see in my head. That's the goal within my work, to get as close as possible to recreating this other world.

Right now, I'm wearing a striped grey and black shirt, my grandfather's tweed jacket and some really thick woolen pants I picked up in Rome for ten bucks. Also some caramely boots made by someone called John Bull. They hurt a bit. In my little leather case I have a book by Irvine Welsh (it's an okay read), a flashing bike light, some discs, pens, deoderant and a pice of amber. This is beginning to sound a little fetishistic. Well, I'm just trying to give you some insight into me!

Other than describing my attire, I study fine arts - majoring in photography as well as other bits and pieces such as multimedia and Indonesian. I love to travel and to barter, especially if it's in a foreign tongue. I live in a big warehouse which is a bit chilly at this time of year but great because you can ride around inside and the cat has plenty of room to play.
