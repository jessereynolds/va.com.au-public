For those of you out there who made me slightly interested in who I am and what I do, here ya go...

I'm a 21-year old country chick turned Melbourne girl making a living doing what I love most. I graduated this year from VUT in Melbourne where I got my Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia and then I sort of fell into my dream job. I'm now working at Tantamount Productions in St Kilda where I help build CD-ROMs and Websites (I do a lil' programming, animating and some artwork) and I'm loving every second of it. My real passion is animation though and since I've discovered Flash I haven't looked back.

Flash enables me to achieve my animation objective because of how inexpensive and easy it is to use. A few years ago I would'nt have been able to afford to experiment with different animation techniques, or perfecting a style of movement simply because it would have been far too expensive and time consuming.

I love to make people laugh so my animations are usually comical. Having a strong storyline is number one to me though, animation second. I think there's a lot of people out there who are hung up on just creating something, anything, in flash and not thinking about engaging the audience. You don't need to be a fantastic artist to tell a good story.

It's pretty sad, but for as passionate as I am about animation I've only ever made one cartoon. I've got this excellent computer and all the software I need but I still need to get my arse into gear. I'd love to develop my characters (which are based on my family) and create more cartoons featuring them. It'll happen....eventually.
