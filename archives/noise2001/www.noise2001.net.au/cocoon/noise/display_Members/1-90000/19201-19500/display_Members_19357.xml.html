blurb  // Aloha! My name's Jordan Lewerissa. I'm a 21 year old illustration major hailing from Brisbane.

As a result of poor maths grades and a steady diet of Coke and pizza, it seems that ever since I was knee high to the proverbial grasshopper, I've been predestined to lead the life of a starving artist. Well, not much has changed - I'm still eating just as unhealthily and I think I stopped growing somewhere around the grasshopper's waist, but over the years, I've been formulating my plan for world-domination. The vehicle with which I hope to execute this plan comes in the form of a foetal-stage graphic novel I've entitled 'Reverie'.

While still in its developmental stages, the book will chronicle the trials and tribulations of my two characters, Zoe and Tryst, and their weird experiences working as mercenaries in a post-apocalyptic wasteland (I'm really scoring on the originality stakes, yes?).

I'd like to say that I draw influence from art-house cinema, classical sculpture and the music of Yanni. However, in actuality, I religiously watch Buffy the Vampire Slayer, read far too many comics and I can't seem to work without Faith No More or Mr Bungle blasting out my eardrums.

The majority of my work is done in pencil, ink and coloured on Adobe Photoshop. I have been known to dabble in other media, however, when one makes as many mistakes as I do, the 'undo' button becomes your child.

I'm also a part-time ninja. 'Strike hard and fade away without a trace' words to live by.
