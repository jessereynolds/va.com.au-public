blurb  // Artist of the day, 11/10/01

// Greetings from the world of a third year artschool student.

// My name is Estelle, I am 21 and my life is photography. I also devote much of my time to design, but photography is me.

// I photograph because I see. I love seeing. I want people to see the world through my eyes and enjoy simplicity. My eye is my personal camera; I constantly look.

I seek meaning through the beauty of looking. Without finding beauty within the everyday I feel lost. Without sight I am unable to capture the magic of my world.

There is a certain joy I receive when I photograph. It might be the light creeping in behind my curtain, or a reflection in a puddle, but when I see it I know I need to photograph it.

I also use photography to capture life. I use it to help me understand the effects of life upon people, including myself. My work is always personal, spurting from the depths of my thought. It is usually emotionally charged, whether it be sorrow, happiness or contemplation.

Photography is the core of my life. I need to photograph what I see because I feel the need to show people my little world. It may be small and inconspicuous, but it exists.

// All of my photography is only that. I do not manipulate in photoshop. I always crop with my eye. What you see is what I see through the lens.

// My influences include Duane Michals, Minor White, Uta Barth among others. My eyesight is my inspiration.

// If you are interested in seeing more of me or my work, please write to estelle@astro-girl.com
