blurb  // My projects are all about..

self-publishing, self-expression and distribution of the creation of likeminded folks, with the DIY ethic being of central importance to the way I do things.

My main zine project is Personality Liberation Front, a pro-feminist punk zine which tries to use critical thought to challenge mainstream society's ideals as well as letting the punk scene go under the microscope, too.

The other part of the PLF Empire is the mailorder distro, which concentrates on disseminating records, zines, books and other small-press propaganda. I also have another zine project- "Her Heroes Aren't Gone" which is a series of letters to grrrl-punk heroes, the first of which is a letter to Kathleen Hanna of the band Bikini Kill.
