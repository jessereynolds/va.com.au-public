blurb  // Artist of the Day, 5/10/01

I'm a Multimedia/Web Designer, In my spare time I also like to create typefaces...
photography,drawing and painting, (usually acrylic on canvas) I love pretty much any creative outlet so I'm constantly using new mediums and even merging formal mediums with the new.

I love to create, it gives me so much pleasure to put effort into something and be happy with the results.

I do it any way I can, at work it's starts with rough sketching and then onto the mac and into a design app. When I'm designing anything it's always best to begin on paper.

I like the fact that I've put my own touch into something and that it can stand alone and say something. I dislike anything being stagnant and I always want to change things.

I'm 23 and lived/studied and worked originally in Perth, about a year ago I made the decision to move to Melbourne to experience what a bigger city could offer me and I think it's paid off.

I have many influences but I'd have to say these are a few of my favourites, Swiss graphic design i.e (Buro Destruct), they have some really great Print based design work and I really like that clean and colourfull
style they have. The Internet, there's heaps of really crappy design on the net but if you know where to look theres also some great stuff i.e (australiainfront, famewhore, surfstation.lu, k10k.com, ). Books and not just design books there's heaps of cool books out there that have influenced the way I approach my design and artwork, some of the books I've been reading lately are titles like, The interpretation of dreams(Sigmund Freud), This is Modern Art(Mathew Collins), B¸ro Destruct (B¸ro Destruct), if I could afford it I'd be buying books everyday. Finally another source of inspiration is what I get from watching people and seeing how they react to things, understanding human behaviour is a very under rated in the design industry.

that pop-up, I can't afford a domain name yet !!
