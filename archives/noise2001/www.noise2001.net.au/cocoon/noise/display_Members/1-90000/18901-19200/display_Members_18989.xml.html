blurb  // I don't know who I am. I know the statistics - 23,
brown hair, blue eyes, born two days after Christmas - but I don't know the truth. And as Oscar Wilde said, "the truth is rarely pure and never simple".

I grew up in Brisbane and Maryborough and am now
making Sydney my home with the aim of pursing some
sort of creative career. I call myself a generalist. I have a science degree and an arts degree and dabble in everything from science communication, to writing erotica. From making short films to acting in them. From photography to graphic design. I find it all too interesting to pick a focus. This is quite frustrating for my parental figures as well as my partner as I struggle to find a full time job that involves enough creativity so I can remain passionate enough to get up in the morning.

My friends say I'm into everything but a bath! Well I debate that - I do bathe often! I manage all this on top of a fairly average office job. My goal is to work fulltime (and get paid) in a creative field and I wouldn't knock fame and fortune if it came my way.
