blurb  // What is intrinsically beautiful about the soul is its ability to communicate its desires, its dreams, its emotions through creative medium.

My aims and insights serve only one purpose: the furtherance of my work, and the progressive evolution of my technical skill.

Why is my work so important to me? Self-satisfaction. To fulfil a desire to turn thoughts, visions, and dreams into something physical, something no longer restricted to theory... to restrain these intangible elements of the psyche within the confines of any given medium.

Creating a temporary dimension wherein each work is an entity unto itself, whether it be in the form of static or moving visual, literary, aural, performance, etc. Where that 'concept' almost develops a soul of its own; sure, easier said than done, but when successful, it's the most incredible sensation you can dare to imagine.

Of course, there is the flip side of the coin where art is created for the enigmatic sake of funáî for innocent self (or collective) amusement, or blatant in-you-face controversialism.

Whatever the cause though, I personally try to take each subject to a new and different level if possible. Whether attempting the creation of something completely new and different, or, and what is more often the case for anyone with a thorough understanding of the nature of creativity, approaching the old with a fresh and unique perspective.

Every true artist is simply a soul in a state of extreme communicative magnification.
