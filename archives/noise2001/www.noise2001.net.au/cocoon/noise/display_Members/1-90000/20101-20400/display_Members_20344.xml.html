blurb  // I am an Industrial design graduate from the University of Canberra.

Here I focused my major studies on contemporary furniture design. This area appeals to me as I find it amazing how people can positively respond to a well designed surrounding environment.

It is apparent that design which considers emotional needs as well as those which are functional will draw a response for those in search of original concepts.

I am influenced by the work of Arne Jacobsen, Charles and Ray Eams, Frank Lloyd Wright, Howard Arkley, Jorg Schmeisser, Jan Flook, Marc Newson, Diesel, Ed (Big Daddy) Roth, and not to mention The Beastie Boys.

It is ultimately my aim to create products which enhance peoples experience in the home and at work. I aim to produce original designs in an attempt to offer people relief from the mundane.

If you want to check out my designs please visit my site.
