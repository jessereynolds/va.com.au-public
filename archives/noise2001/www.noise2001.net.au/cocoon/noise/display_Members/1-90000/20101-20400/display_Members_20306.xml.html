blurb  // Artist of the Day, 13/10/01.

I'm me. I like Australian music because I'm more likely to see the musicians involved in it on a stage than I am to see American or Italian or Pakistani, or any other nation's musicians, plus it's made in the place where I live and I can relate to it because of that.

I like Australian films for similiar reasons, but any honest bit of film making is fine by me.

I write, constantly, 2 poems every day for four years now, and I make songs with an ingenious piano player I know as often as we possibly can.

I also make textile art and dislike immensely the lack of recognition that textile art receives when so much of it is truly amazing stuff, (not including my own, that'd just be a bit of foolish pride).

Animation is also something that compells me to get completely lost in it and hopefully I'll eventually have the skills that I need to be a fairly good animator.

In the future I hope to make loads and loads of interesting bits of stuff and teach other people to do the same. That's the existence I'm after.

E-WORKS-My website is about self improvement through surgical means going a bit too far.Up untill the 11th page when it starts to get a bit crazy and make absolutely no sense whatsoever. It's all animated, apart from one page and it shows people what goes on inside my head, things have some sort of connection to each other at first, but eventually that stops.I'm trying to make people smile a bit, the idea is that some doctor's worked out a way to surgically attach velcro to the human body so that peoples limbs become interchangeable.


Check out more of Corey's work, read more about him and meet a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Corey is the Artist of the Day, 13/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
