This same yolk was later involved in a controversial egg heist, in which it was shipped off to suburbia in a neatly packaged egg shell. After hatching in January, it soon grew to accept a new human life, and a family which loved it despite it's fowl appearance. (ha)

This particular specimen, now 18 years of age, soon became obsessed with the process of scribbling lines onto a piece of bleached tree, to alleviate confusion in the juicy brain.

It now spends it's time at art school making contraptions out of wool and paint, publishing zines, and plans to one day, be the first chicken in history to fully immerse a popular landmark in a steaming hot paste of canned spaghetti.
