My name is Daniel King, I'm originally from Sydney, and I've been working in film TV for 3 years.

I first got involved in the industry when I worked on a student VCA (Victorian College of the Arts) short film titled "Unfinished Business", starring Aaron Pedersen and Ernie Dingo.

Studying video production at RMIT, my first film "Identity" (1999) won best documentary at the 9th RMIT Film Festival.

My latest film "The Opportunists" screened at 2001 St Kilda Film Festival and will screen at Short Stuff at the Melbourne Fringe Festival this year.

I am currently in pre-production on my third film, which has received funding from the Foundation of Young Australians.


Synopsis of The Opportunists

Small time crims in the backblocks of Northcote. Dodgy backdoor deals.
Big time Aboriginal art scams. Mates become enemies. Culture becomes commerce. A day in the life of an Aboriginal painting on the run.
So who are the Opportunists?
