blurb  // Artist of the day, 4/10/01

I am in my 3rd year of a BFA. The biggest thing that inspires me would have to be people. What makes them tick, what makes them get up in the morning, what makes them silent and what makes them make noise? It is the random things about humans and the obscure things that makes me fasinated. The patterns they make and the information they need to survive. Do they need all the information, or can they make a decision based on their own intuition?

My final year work is based on humans and the information they need. Some people are dummed down by having too much information given to them, they get used to this, and so rely on always having everything given to them. I feel it is what people don't say that is understood, and I am looking at this. By taking away, such as a person's features, you are just left with their outline, but what can you work out from this information? Probably a lot more than you realised.
