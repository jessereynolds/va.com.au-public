blurb  // Ever since I was little I've enjoyed doing all things crafty.

The only difference now is that I'm receiving a degree for my artistic endeavors! Even then, as now the main focus of my creations was the animal world. Having grown up with several pets, including every girls dream pet, a horse, I was fascinated by the range of colours, textures and shapes of animals. I found people very dull, I would spend hours constructing little horses out of pipe cleaners and show jumping courses for them to compete on.

Today my methods are a slightly more sophisticated (only slightly, considering the material I use most is paper mache) but are still dealing with my interest in the animal world.
