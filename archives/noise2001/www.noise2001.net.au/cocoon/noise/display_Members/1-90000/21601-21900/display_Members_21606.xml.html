blurb  // I think a lot, sometimes too much and over-analyse everything.

I'm not the guy behind the guy behind the guy, I'm probably the guy who just dropped his change all over the floor across the room.

I like to observe people. I think we are the strangest creatures on this planet, but I like being part of that.

A lot of the inspiration for the work I do comes from films, comics, music videos and just what I see going on in the street and in peoples lives. I always carry a notebook and a pen to write down ideas for interviewing or music videos.

My other inspiration is music and it is essential in my life, it gives my mind the freedom to wander and explore new realms.

I got really interested in photography ages ago in school, but I never did anything about it. Then a year or ago my dad brought me a digital camera which I took everywhere. I took a lot of photos on trains. I really like the look of trains and back streets, urban jungle stuff.

My girlfriend Tamaiya was my main inspiration to keep going with photography. She's a photographer herself and her work inspires me, its amazing work and she's also my
perfect model. She told me about noise, so I started taking more and more photos with her SLR camera.

Since I'd only worked on digital it was really exciting and I got really into it and had a good time. noise seemed like a great opportunity to get something published since I've never had anything published before.

My tips for people undertaking something like this in the future is to give it all you got. Stop thinking about it, cause if you don't try you'll never know.


People from around the country submitted photos that reflected their life and their world. A selection of shots that reflect the Black + White aesthetic will be published in the October edition of Black + White Magazine.
