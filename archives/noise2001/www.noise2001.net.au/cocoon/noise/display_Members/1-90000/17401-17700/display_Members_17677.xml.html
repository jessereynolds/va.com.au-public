blurb  // I am 23 and currently studying a Bachelor of Multimedia in Brisbane. I also write and perform electronic music for the Zonar label and between these two pursuits, spend way too much time in front of computers ~~~

I started getting into computers on a creative level in 1997 doing digital image kinda stuff. In 1999 I started writing electronic music and became interested in other forms of multimedia. Then in 2000 I started the Multimedia degree, and began developing in other areas like web design, programming and interactive video

I see digital technology as an exciting medium through which new and unique types of expression and interaction can occur. Although a lot of what I do is web-related, the internet to me is more a means of making digital media accessible to large numbers of people, and moving past immediate geography as a basis for the creation of communities, and its for these reasons that I put a lot of my work online. The net itself in my opinion has no intrinsic creative potential, and is at the moment largely a corporate orientated medium.
