blurb  // Artist of the day, 1/10/01

I am a Brisbane based emerging artist working in the areas of performance, installation and new media. To date I have participated in various group exhibitions including Switch at the Palace Gallery in Brisbane, and Un-Re(a)d, a multimedia fusion of text and imagery launched at the Adelaide International Fringe Festival.

Over the past three years I have also travelled the country with the Post Hoc Performing Word Company, performing at The Newcastle Writers Festival

and the Queensland Writers Festival. In 2000 I was appointed director of Post Hoc and co-ordinated Where the City Keeps Its Secrets, a spoken word performance showcased in Brisbane at The Subverse Poetry Festival.

Currently completing a Bachelor of Creative Arts at Griffith University, I am particularly interested in cross-disciplinary art forms, and the relationship between writing, visual arts and theoretical discourse.


Read more about Sarah-Mace, view more of her work, and check out a whole new generation of visual and craft artists on the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.
