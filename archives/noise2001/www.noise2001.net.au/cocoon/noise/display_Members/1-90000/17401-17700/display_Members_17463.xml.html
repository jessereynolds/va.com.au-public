blurb  // My interests are basically Graphic Design and Hip Hop.
(not necessarily in that order).

I work in the former field as a junior designer for an advertising agency where most of my time is spent producing retail ads. This can actually be seen as a good thing as it motivates me to work on my own personnel projects - cue Hip-Hop.

I'm active in 3 of the 4 elements of Hip-Hop; Graffiti, DJing and Rhyming and I contribute to the scene on a regular basis via all three.
As DJ Mexi I play at Canberra's weekly Hip-Hop & Breaks night Fracture. Before it's closure on New Years Eve 2000/01 I was a resident at Heaven, playing weekly at Criminal Minded and monthly at Ill Repute. All of these events were/are co-hosted by my friends and fellow members of the Four Storey Empire - a collective of like minded Hip-Hop heads dedicated to promoting and encouraging the Canberra Hip-Hop scene (and ourselves of course!).]

As an M.C. I perform with the Hospice Crew. Originally formed in Canberra by fellow M.C. Ciecmate the crew has expanded to Melbourne where Ciecmate, Newsense and Smokey Brindo are attracting much deserved attention!
If I'm not actively partaking in it, then I'm helping to promote Canberra's small yet dedicated Hip-Hop community via fliers and club events.
The majority of this work is produced in Illustrator with a little help from Photoshop, Streamline and my retractable pencil!
