blurb  // Artist of the day, 23/10/01

My name is Don Dixon and I am 19 years old. I come from Warren where I attended school till Year 10. I found work on the CDEP at Warren before moving to Narromine. I found myself doing art which I am still doing and enjoying it.

On my Dad's side my totem is the goanna from the Wangkamara tribe situated in southwest Qld. On my Mum's side my totem is the fish from the Macquarie River which she and her family grew up around. My mother was born and raised on a reserve better known as the Beemunnell.

See some of Don's work, read more about him and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Don is the Artist of the Day, 23/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
