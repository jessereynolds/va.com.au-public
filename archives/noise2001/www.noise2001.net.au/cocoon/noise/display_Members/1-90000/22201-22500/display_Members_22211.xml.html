blurb  // Belinda is 23, a strong young Murri woman, who is employed by the Brisbane Indigenous Media Assoication.

The association runs a 24 hour Murri and country music radio station 4 AAA. Belinda has been employed with the station for the past five years as an announcer and programmer. She has broadcast major festivals includin the Woodford Folk Festival, Gympie Muster and Tamworth Festival, and was a member of the Indigenous broadcast teams at the Sydney 2000 Olympics and Corroboree 2000.


Catch Belinda's program 'Young Murri Women Singin' It Up', on 4AAA, 98.9 FM in October.

In conjunction with Arts Queensland, as part of the Queensland Government's cultural policy for young people, noise has commissioned $20,000 worth of radio production by young Queensland radio producers for the festival.

Tune in to community radio stations across Queensland for 'noise live' special programs, featuring local young artists, and listen out for pre-produced features throughout the month.
