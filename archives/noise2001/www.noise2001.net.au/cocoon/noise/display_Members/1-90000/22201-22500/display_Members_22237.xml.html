blurb  // Artist of the day, 31/10/01

In 1996 I completed a design course at TAFE that led me to study Fine Arts at the National Art School.

I graduated from the national art school majoring in painting in 1999.

After winning a painting prize in 1998 I was able to travel to Europe and see many of great galleries in England, France and Spain. On returning I felt very enthusiastic about painting and have since had the opportunity to be a finalist in the inaugural Brett Whiteley Travelling Art Scholarship, have some of my work purchased by both private and commercial collectors and work with a number of professional artists on various installations and projects mostly in Sydney.

I now live in Melbourne and am preparing for an exhibition in late January, 2002.

See some of Jason's work, read more about him and check out a new generation of visual and craft artists at the National Association for the Visual Arts' cool website An Artist a Day.

Jason is the Artist of the Day, 31/10/01. For more on this project see An Artist a Day at noise.
