real name  // Laura Vines 
blurb  // The whimsical fights the melancholic, the lyrical bleeds into satire - Laura's writing springs off the paper and into the mind sometimes with a whisper, oft with a smack! An actress, script-writer, poet and spoken word artist, 21 year old Laura Vines has been published in the Noise festivalís Anthology and True Tales of Love and Hate, as well as zines such as Mod Piece and Internal Bleedings. She has been the featured performer at spoken word nights such as Babble, The Spinning Wheel and Passionate Tongues. Lauraís spoken word was distributed with Voiceworks on the Mouthpiece CD. Her short plays She Dances by Night (starring and co-directed by Laura Vines) and Trauma Haunting have been performed at the Carlton Courthouse in conjunction with La Mama Theatre and Melbourne Universityís Mudfest. She is currently in Dublin performing at the International Bloomsday Festival. Her work is diverse, fresh and ready to ricochet across the country!
