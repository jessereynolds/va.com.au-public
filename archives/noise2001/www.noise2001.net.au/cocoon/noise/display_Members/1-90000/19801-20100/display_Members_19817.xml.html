blurb  // We'll have to make this one short and sweet.

Basically i'm a qualified/freelancing graphic designer, current multimedia trainee, avid photographer, painter, and passionate fashion designer (I make handbags, clothing and jewellery).

i am most influenced by the beautiful design work of the collective Tomato ( UK). their images, type, and philosophy are truly inspirational.

i'm also inspired by the evocative illustrations of Hiroshi Tanabe, the retro illustrations of 1950's pin-up girls, the elegance of the 1920's, and the paintings of Waterhouse.

as for fashion, the designers who inspire me most are Comme des Garcons, Michelle Jank, Akira, [ I ] Peck Your Pun, Karen Walker, Sample, Alanna Hill, Gorman, Miyake, PAM, Galliano and Chalayan.

i also find myself constantly inspired by my surroundings, family, friends and music.

as for my actual work, i would describe it as quite minimalist, simple, elegant, clean, and incorporating as much negative space and sans serif type as i can get.

the whole process usually begins with a photograph that i've taken (or with fashion, an idea that just happens to form in my mind). i then go about the creative process. so i use a lot of photography as well as ink drawings.

and lastly why do i do all of this?? well, it's release, it's an essential part of my life, as well as a satisfying process....
