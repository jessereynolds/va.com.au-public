festival projects
In early 2001, noise opened up more than 25 projects for public submission from Australia's young creatives. Thousands of submissions later, we had ourselves a mighty fine festival on our hands.

Here's where you'll find the avenues to view all projects and the artists and works associated with them. Enjoy!
