NEW MEDIA ARTS and THE AUSTRALIA COUNCIL

Location: Paddington RSL, Sydney, Australia
Date and Time: Monday 24 January 2005, 6pm (GMT+1100) (other timezones)
Duration: 2 hrs

Listen Live: QuickTime Audio (suitable for modem users) or
Watch Live: QuickTime MPEG4 Audio and Video (suitable for broadband - ADSL or cable etc)

Alternatively: Listen or Watch Live using RTSP url. Note the RTSP url may work for you if the above QuickTime links fail.

An open forum to discuss the Australia Council for the Arts' recent announcement of a major restructure, including the abolition of both the Community Cultural Development Board and the New Media Arts Board. Convened by dLux Media Arts and ANAT in association with Realtime, Performance Space, Experimenta, BEAP and MAAP.
