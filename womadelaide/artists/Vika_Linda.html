Vika & Linda

Tonga - Australia

Sisters Vika & Linda Bull have provided one of the freshest sounds of 1994, conquering the charts with the hit song We've Started a Fire and now set for more success with their new single, Hard Love. Beautiful harmonies and joyful performances are their trademarks - a simply inspirational combination!
