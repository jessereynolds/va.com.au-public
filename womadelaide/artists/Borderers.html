The Borderers

Australia

The Borderers are a wily collective of musicians whose hybrid folk/country/pop sounds, and energetic catchy songs have made the Australian and English music industries sit up and take notice. Their debut album 'Heritage' has won a string of awards and with the bundle of talent contained in this one band, their future looks very bright. The Borderers are: top Australian songwriter Terry Bradford, Jim Paterson, (UK Band, Dexy's Midnight Runners), Alex, (Shamrock and Thistle), Jeff Algra, (Seven Stories), Richard Tonkin, (Kelly's Revenge and Spiral Dance) and Rod Boothroyd. See them before the world snaps them up! The Borderers appear with the support of Foundation SA.
