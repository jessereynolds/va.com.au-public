Lokua Kanza

Zaire

Zairean music remains best-known for its guitar-driven rhythms, but Lokua Kanza, former lead guitarist for Reine Abeti and an arranger for Papa Wemba, has brought vocals to the fore. His breathtaking use of voice mixes chants and a capella with a light percussive background. Now based in Paris, Lokua joins with male and female singers to make a sound that celebrates the full emotional range of the human voice in an inventive rhythmic style that is unlike anything else to come out of Zaire.
