Kate Ceberano

Australia

She is Australia's queen of soul. With a new single due out in February and an album following in March, WOMADELAIDE audiences will get a sneak preview of Kate's new work - a smooth fusion of soul, jazz and contemporary new sounds. Kate has recently signed a world-wide deal with Elektra Records in New York, so this may be a rare opportunity to see the gal before she flies off into the realms of stellar-stardom.
