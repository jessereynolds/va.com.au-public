Christine Anu

Australia

Christine Anu's single Monkey and the Turtle entered the charts as a hit, with her new release, My Island Home from her forthcoming album 'INNA ANU S.T.Y.L.E', set to follow suit. Her music fuses urban dance grooves, traditional Island stories and samples, dialect and funky guitar riffs over a slap bass sound. Add to all this one sweet, soulful and powerful voice and you've got a potent new sound. An AIDT dance graduate and former performer with Bangarra, Christine Anu is an artist on the verge of something very big!
