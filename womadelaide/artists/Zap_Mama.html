Zap Mama

Zaire

Zap Mama capture hearts wherever they appear. Their joyous a capella mix of Pygmy music, Central African and Arabic pop tunes, human beatbox sounds, AfroCuban rhythms with a touch of Soul and Gospel, is unique by any standards. With beautiful five-part harmonies, their entertaining, humorous and energetic performances are truly stunning.
