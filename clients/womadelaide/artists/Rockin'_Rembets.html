The Rockin' Rembets


Greece/Australia

The Rockin Rembets perform impassioned Greek rebetiko music, often referred to as the Greek Blues! Their music expresses the experiences of people living on urban margins of mainstream society and draws on music from Greece, Turkey and the Middle East. Using traditional instruments including the bouzouki, violin, baglama, guitar, toumberleki, the Rockin Rembets draw from a rich tradition of songs, lively dance rhythms and inspired improvisations.
The Rockin' Rembets appear with the support of Foundation SA.
