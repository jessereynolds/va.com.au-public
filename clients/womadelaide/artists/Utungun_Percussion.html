Utungun Percussion

Australia

This Sydney-based five-piece band produces mesmerising rhythms, creating music which draws from many indigenous cultures from around the world. Often dressed in wild high hats and usually working the crowd up to a rhythmic frenzy, the troupe is led by Greg Sheehan - regarded as one of Australia's finest drummer/percussionists. A stunning array of drums and percussion instruments, combine with voice and movement to create an effect of wonderful compulsive energy.
