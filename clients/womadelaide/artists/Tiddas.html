Tiddas

Australia

Since their debut WOMADELAIDE appearance in 1993, audiences around the world have been captivated by these Aboriginal/Celtic sisters. Having played WOMAD festivals in Bath and Reading, London's Southbank Festival and Amsterdam's Ruigoord Festival in 1993, they have made 1994 just as successful with the release of their hit album 'Sing About Life'. Tiddas' sweet harmonies and natural sense of enthusiasm and exuberance have made them one of the most popular and well-loved groups in Australia - and very welcome return guests for WOMADELAIDE 95.
