ALT

Andy White - Liam O'Maonlia - Tim Finn

Ireland - Ireland - New Zealand

A superb combination of three internationally acclaimed singer/songwriters comes together to form ALT. Tim Finn - the highly successful solo artist and former front man of Split Enz - needs no introduction to Australian audiences. Liam O' Maonlai is the lead singer of the Hot House Flowers and Andy White is regarded as one of Ireland's finest songwriters. The three first came together to write Many's the Time on Tim's 'Before and After' album and liked the combo so much they decided to do it again. Their first single is We're all Men from the 'ALT' album - listen out for it, then come and see them live.
