Bangarra Dance Theatre

Australia

Internationally acclaimed Bangarra Dance Theatre is one of Australia's most respected and innovative dance companies. Through the genius of Artistic Director Stephen Page, Bangarra has spearheaded the development of a new style of contemporary Australian dance, drawing boldly on the essence and spirituality of Australian indigenous culture. With numerous national awards and accolades world-wide, Bangarra has become an inspiring creative force for all indigenous and non-indigenous Australians.
