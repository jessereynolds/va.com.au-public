Kona Lien

South America - Australia

There is nothing more irresistible than the sound of panpipes. This band of traditional singers and instrumentalists create the magical music of the Andes. From the wistful sounds of the panpipes to the high pitched resonance of the ten-stringed charango, this spiritually rich and earthy music speaks of the lives, joys and pain of the indigenous people of South America. Now based in Adelaide, this troupe of artists comes together from New Zealand, Mexico, Iran and Chile to create a joyous celebration of one of the world's great music traditions.
Kona - Lien appears with the support of Foundation SA.
