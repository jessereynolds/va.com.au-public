Rough Image

Australia

Local reggae-rock band, Rough Image is one of Australia's newest exports following a tour to the United States last year. Currently un-signed, yet with a number of labels hot on the trail, Rough Image's demo tapes are being given huge air-play on Native American and African American airwaves. Drawing on the rich Ngarrindjeri heritage, Rough Image's music speaks of unity and togetherness underpinned by energetic dance grooves.
Rough Image appears with the support of Foundation SA.
