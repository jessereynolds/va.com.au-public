Archie Roach &
Ruby Hunter

Australia

Archie Roach and Ruby Hunter are one of Australia's most respected singer/songwriter teams. Often cited by other musicians as a source of great inspiration, both Archie and Ruby have become legends in the music industry. Archie's critically acclaimed 'Charcoal Lane' was swiftly followed by the success of 'Jamu Dreaming'. Ruby Hunter's first solo album 'Thoughts Within' is an inspiring and insightful collection of work which has brought high praise from music critics throughout Australia. Powerful music straight from the heart.
